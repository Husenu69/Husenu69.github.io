Mold Inspection Services

Our home can quickly become infested with mold if a source of water is present. Mold can spread throughout your home in as little as 48 hours. Since mold can produce allergens and irritants, you should call us to inspect your home if you suspect you have a mold problem.Every mold damage scenario is different and requires a unique solution. However, in general, the mold remediation process stays the same. We use advanced mold remediation techniques and equipment to professionally remediate your residential or commercial mold infestation.

Comprehensive Professional Snagging Surveys

Did you know more than half of new-build homes have major faults and snags including problems with construction, fittings and utilities. Left uncorrected within your new propertys warranty period these issues could cost you thousands to fix. SwiftSnagg Solutions provide comprehensive home snagging surveys to customers across much of the UK from our bases on the West Midlands .Our professional home snaggers provide you with detailed visual snag lists and advise on your properties legal warranty rights.

Heat-Loss Surveys

We have been carrying out thermal heat loss surveys on new build properties for the past 7 years and have discovered hundreds of houses with missing cavity wall insulation, missing or poorly laid loft insulation, thermal bridging, draughts, inefficient heating systems and cold air infiltration.

Aerial Drone Surveys

We check externally and internally to assess the condition of any roofing system and assess for any signs structural damage or poor workmanship. Thorough inspections will determine both whether any cosmetic repair work is required alongside assessing any potential underlying structural issues. Using our skills and experience we look for any defects however minor which are currently causing water ingress problems or have the potential to lead to problems in the future.
