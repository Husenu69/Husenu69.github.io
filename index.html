Services


Interior painting: Painting the walls, ceilings, trim, and other surfaces inside a house. Exterior painting: Painting the exterior walls, trim, and other surfaces of a house. Deck and fence painting or staining: Painting or staining wooden decks and fences to protect them from the elements. Color consultation: Helping clients choose the right colors for their painting projects. Pressure washing: Using a high-pressure water spray to clean and prepare surfaces before painting. Wallpaper removal: Removing old wallpaper to prepare surfaces for painting. Drywall repair: Repairing cracks, holes, and other damage to drywall before painting. Cabinet refinishing: Refinishing and painting kitchen cabinets and other cabinetry. Textured painting: Creating decorative finishes such as stucco or popcorn ceilings .The specific services provided by a house painter may vary depending on their skills and experience, as well as the needs of their clients. A professional house painter should be able to provide a consultation and estimate based on the client's needs and the scope of the project.

Before the job

Our company is  focuses on protecting your property, flooring, and furniture with plastic sheeting and builders' paper.

Our care

Vents, wall plates, blinds will be removed before painting. We make the necessary repairs like patches, holes, cracks.
We caulk around door casings, frames, baseboards and we do clean lines.

Client satisfaction

Paint&Decor move your possessions and furniture back to the original place. Finally, we walk you through the entire project area to ensure you are completely satisfied.

Portfolio


Paint&Decor provides a level 5 finish, a high-quality skim coat over walls or ceilings, creating a pleasing, completed look.
We can apply a texture to match existing, or in most cases, we use orange peel or knockdown texture.
