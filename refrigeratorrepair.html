Warning Signs

Does your fridge need service?

Catching problems early usually means a smaller repair bill. Watch for these signs.

  • Food spoils faster than normal
  • Refrigerator runs continuously
  • Excessive condensation inside the unit
  • Unusual clicking, humming, or grinding sounds
  • Water pooling beneath the appliance
  • Freezer temperature fluctuations
  • Ice maker stopped producing ice
  • Door seals not closing properly
  • Unexpected increase in energy bills

Preventive Care

Keep your refrigerator running longer

Simple habits that reduce wear on critical components and improve energy efficiency.

  1. Clean condenser coils every six months to maintain efficiency.
  2. Inspect door gaskets regularly — worn seals let cold air escape.
  3. Keep vents and air passages unobstructed inside the fridge.
  4. Set and maintain the correct temperature (37°F fridge / 0°F freezer).
  5. Avoid overloading shelves, which blocks airflow.
  6. Replace water filters on schedule — usually every 6 months.
  7. Listen for new or unusual operating sounds and address them early.
