What we see most

Common appliance issues in Williamsburg

These are the issues we fix most often for Williamsburg homeowners.

01

Refrigerators not cooling properly

A warm fridge puts your food at risk fast. We pinpoint the cause — compressor, thermostat, condenser coils, or refrigerant — and fix it on the first visit before spoilage becomes a problem.

02

Washing machines not spinning or draining

A washer stuck mid-cycle is a laundry emergency. We handle door lock faults, clogged pumps, worn belts, and control board failures — getting your machine back in service quickly.

03

Dryers not heating

A dryer that runs but won't heat is almost always a fixable part failure — heating element, thermal fuse, or gas valve. We diagnose accurately so you're not paying for parts you don't need.

04

Dishwashers not cleaning effectively

Poor wash results usually point to a clogged spray arm, failing pump, or water inlet issue. We restore full cleaning performance so you're not hand-washing every load.
